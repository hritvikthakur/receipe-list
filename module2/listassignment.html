• Spaghetti Recipie
    1. Ingredients
      • Spaghetti
      • Marinara Sauce
      • Salt
      • Water
    2. Instructions
      1. Bring water to a boil.
      2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
        1. Add salt to taste.
        2. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring ocassionally.
      3. Add marinara sauce to pan, bringing to asimmer.
      4. Mixed cooked spaghetti to marinara sauce.
      5. Enjoy.
